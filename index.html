The Brookline Scavenger Hunt

A competition against joylessness! Foster curiosity. Explore Brookline.

How to Play

Rules: Do it whenever, at your leisure, with any size group you wish. It's untimed and no sweat!

The Reward: Complete a category to win a unique-in-the-world token (specifically designed for this hunt with a hole for a necklace string). Finish all four to become a Brookline Legend!

How to Submit: Send photos, videos, or links to a shared folder to nsmader@gmail.com. We judge "easy-breezy"—we just want to see the spirit of the clue captured!

📍 Places

  • Take pictures of the Boston Skyline from 3 different spots in Brookline.
  • Find the location for What3Words: Bond.grant.herb.
  • Visit every Elementary School and pose as their mascot.
  • Find the final resting place of Mary Boyleston (b. 1648).
  • Find the Brookline "path" with the most steps; take a team photo at the top.
  • Find where Bach's Toccata in C was performed on Feb 16, 1900.

🌳 Nature

  • Identify and photograph these trees: American Sycamore, Linden, and Cypress.
  • Find a 2-foot square patch with 5 different species visible at once.
  • Photobomb a Brookline Turkey (safely!).
  • Document the entire length of the Emerald Necklace within town limits.
  • Paint or draw a still-life of vegetables from Allandale Farm.

👥 People

  • Find a Brookline resident with a team member's first name and the last name Schwartz, Johnson, Patel, Rivera, or Lee. Give them a gift!
  • Spell a word using human bodies on a grassy field.
  • Bring a baked treat to the Brookline Select Board.
  • Conduct a 2-question survey with 10 locals and chart the results.
  • Introduce yourself to a Barista, Construction Worker, Crossing Guard, Librarian, Spiritual Leader, and a Puppeteer. Ask for a local recommendation!

🎨 Activities & Things

  • Design a flag for Brookline (think simple and symbolic).
  • Build a model of Brookline architecture using food.
  • Create a 1-minute choreographed video or stop-motion clip about a Brookline fact.
  • The "Fusion Recipe": Combine ingredients found at Paris Creperie and Mike's Deli into a new home-cooked dish.
  • Make a three-story human pyramid in front of a town building.
